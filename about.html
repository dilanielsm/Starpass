Our Story

Star Pass is a higher education services provider that runs all their courses online. From business to accounting to hospitality, Star Pass is home to over 200 professional and academic courses.

With over two decades of experience in the higher education industry and over a decade of experience in delevering courses online, the team behind Star Pass look out for the best interest of their student base.

We are a proud subsidiary of the London School of Education Group. Our main focus is to deliver accredited courses across the globe to students that are looking for qualifications to suit their convenience.

Our intention is to make education meaningful,
interactive and enjoyable for our students.

Our Take On E - Learning

E-learning should be a rewarding as well as an engainging experience for learners. It is important that content is readily available for students to access at their convenience.

The blended learning concept should take precedent for all learners, to ensure that they are not restricted to a single and minimalist learning approach, which could lead to an unpleasent learning experience.

Keeping up with the trends of e-learning 2.0 technology, Star Pass deliver courses though a state of the art virtual learning environment, designed and developed by Edappy.

Edappy is also a subsidiary of the London School of Education Group. The technology backing and the student learning experience has been the forefront of Edappy’s focus since it’s inception back in 2012.
